Pricing & Subscription FAQ

Common questions about plans, billing, and subscriptions 💳

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), as well as EFT and SnapScan for South African customers. All payments are processed securely.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly billing? +

Yes! You can upgrade to yearly billing at any time to get the 25% discount. If you switch from yearly to monthly, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

What happens when I reach my resource limit? +

On the Free plan (5 resources/week), you'll need to wait until the next week resets. On the Pro plan (200 resources/month), you can either wait until next month or contact us to temporarily increase your limit if needed.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Absolutely! There are no long-term commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your paid period.

Do you offer refunds? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact us for a full refund, no questions asked.

How does the Schools plan work? +

The Schools plan is customized for institutions. Pricing is R399 per teacher per month, with volume discounts available. You get an admin dashboard, centralized billing, shared resource library, and priority support. Contact us for a custom quote.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Yes! You can upgrade from Free to Pro at any time and start using premium features immediately. If you downgrade from Pro to Free, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases? +

Yes! Schools purchasing for 10+ teachers receive volume discounts. NGOs and under-resourced schools may qualify for special pricing. Contact us to discuss your needs.

Is VAT included in the pricing? +

All prices shown are inclusive of 15% South African VAT where applicable. Your invoice will show the VAT breakdown.

Will my pricing change after I subscribe? +

Your subscription price is locked in when you sign up. Any price changes will only affect new subscribers, and we'll notify existing customers well in advance if changes occur.
