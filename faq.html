Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using UfundiAI 💡

Getting started

How does UfundiAI work? +

UfundiAI uses advanced AI to generate CAPS and ATP-aligned teaching materials. Simply select your phase, grade, subject, term, and week from our structured dropdowns, then choose what type of material you need (lesson plan, worksheet, PPT, or assessment). The AI creates professional, curriculum-compliant materials in seconds!

Is it easy to get started with UfundiAI? +

Absolutely! Just sign up, select your grade and subject, and you can start generating materials immediately. No training needed - our intuitive interface guides you through every step.

Can we try UfundiAI before subscribing? +

Yes! Our Free plan lets you create 5 resources every week with no credit card required. Try it out and upgrade when you're ready.

Features & planning

Is UfundiAI suitable for all teaching staff? +

Yes! UfundiAI works for all grades (R-12) and all CAPS subjects. Whether you're a foundation phase teacher or FET specialist, we've got you covered.

How much time does UfundiAI save teachers? +

Teachers report saving 10-15 hours per week on lesson planning and material preparation. What used to take hours now takes minutes!

Can UfundiAI adapt lessons for different student needs? +

Yes! You can customize the complexity level and topic focus to match your learners' needs. For worksheets, you can even generate multiple versions for differentiation (Support, Core, Extension levels).

Curriculum support

Is UfundiAI curriculum-aligned? −

100% yes! Every material generated is aligned with the official CAPS curriculum and follows the Annual Teaching Plan (ATP) week-by-week structure. We ensure complete compliance with DBE requirements.

What grades/years and subjects are supported by UfundiAI? +

We support all grades from Grade R through Grade 12, covering all CAPS subjects including Mathematics, Languages, Natural Sciences, Social Sciences, Life Sciences, Physical Sciences, and more.

Pricing & trials

What does UfundiAI cost? +

We offer three plans: Free (R0 - 5 resources/week), Pro (R79/month - 200 resources/month), and Schools (custom pricing for institutions). See our Pricing page for full details.

What's included in UfundiAI Pro? +

Pro includes 200 resources/month, curriculum alignment, lesson series up to 12 lessons, presentations up to 25 slides, removal of UfundiAI branding, YouTube video integration, and all 11 SA languages.

Is there a free version of UfundiAI for teachers? +

Yes! Our Free plan is completely free forever. Create 5 resources every week with no credit card required. Perfect for trying out the platform.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Security & compliance

Is UfundiAI secure and data compliant? +

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and follow POPIA compliance guidelines. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Does UfundiAI store student data? +

No. UfundiAI only stores the teaching materials you generate. We don't collect or store any student information or personal data about learners.
